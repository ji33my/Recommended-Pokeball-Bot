POKÉBALL BOT SETUP

Add the bot to OBS in under 2 minutes. You only do this once.

1

Open OBS

Open OBS Studio. You can do this setup while not streaming — it works anytime.

2

Add a Browser Source

In your Sources panel, click the + button and choose Browser.
Name it something like PokéBall Bot and click OK.

3

Paste the URL

In the URL field, paste this address:

https://YOUR-GITHUB-USERNAME.github.io/YOUR-REPO-NAME/

Set Width to 560 and Height to 640. Click OK.

4

Enter your channel name

The bot page will load inside OBS. Type your Twitch channel name (without the #) and click START.

Your channel name is saved automatically — you'll never need to type it again.

5

You're done!

The bot is now running. Every time OBS opens, the bot starts automatically.
You don't need to click anything — it remembers your channel.

You can minimize or hide the source — it still works in the background.

Tip: If the bot ever stops responding, right-click the Browser Source in OBS and choose Refresh. It will reconnect automatically.
